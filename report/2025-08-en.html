The company's technology enables enterprises to maintain comprehensive oversight of their SaaS application landscape while ensuring robust security measures.

Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) is a framework that ensures the right individuals have the appropriate access to technology resources for legitimate purposes. 2. Axiom Secure Access Management Solutions

On 26th August 2025, identity and access management solutions provider Okta (NASDAQ: OKTA) agreed to acquire Israeli startup Axiom in a deal valued at $100 million, enhancing its cloud-based identity and permissions management capabilities while expanding its research and development presence in Israel.

Axiom, established in 2021, has developed an innovative identity-based platform for permissions and access management specifically engineered for cloud environments, SaaS applications, and databases. The company has secured $10 million in funding from prominent cybersecurity investors, including executives from Netflix, Armis, and Demisto.

Okta, which maintains a market capitalization of approximately $16 billion, specializes in identity and access management solutions. This strategic acquisition represents its second significant investment in Israel, following the acquisition of Spera in December 2023, which established its initial presence in the region.

The integration of Axiom's technology strengthens Okta's implementation of Zero Trust Security principles through advanced Just-in-Time access management and Least Privilege protocols. The acquisition particularly enhances Okta's Privileged Access Management (PAM) offerings, enabling organizations to strengthen their security infrastructure while maintaining operational efficiency. Axiom's team will be integrated into Okta's Israeli development center, further consolidating the company's technical expertise in identity security solutions.

Privileged Access Management (PAM) is a security framework that controls and monitors access to critical systems and sensitive information by users with elevated permissions. PAM solutions help organizations enforce security policies, mitigate the risk of unauthorized access, and maintain compliance with regulations by implementing measures such as session recording, password management, and access control.
